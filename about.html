Wouldn't you want to know how the produce you buy impacts the environment? By being more seasonally conscious of the fruits and vegetables that we purchase, we embrace sustainable shopping methods, support local farmers, and choose better eating habits! InSeason is simple: the next time you go grocery shopping, input your groceries into our app/website, and receive a report that informs you of which items are not in season. By utilizing InSeason's techniques for helping users make more mindful consumer decisions, you contribute towards a healthier planet.
